- Requests to ship internationally (scam)
- Offers to "send a shipper" without seeing it (scam)

**Good buyer signals:**
- Mentions their tow vehicle
- Asks technical questions about specific systems
- Mentions prior overland experience
- Located within reasonable driving distance
- Timeline of 1-4 weeks
